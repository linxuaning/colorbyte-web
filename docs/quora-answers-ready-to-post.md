## Answer #8

**Question:** "How to restore water damaged photos?"

**Answer:**
```
Water damaged photos are challenging but often recoverable. Here's what to do:

**If Photos Are Still Wet:**
1. Don't separate stuck photos (you'll tear them)
2. Rinse gently in clean water to remove mud/debris
3. Lay flat on absorbent paper to dry
4. Air dry completely before scanning (no heat!)
5. Freezing can prevent further damage if you can't dry immediately

**For Already Dried Photos:**

**Physical Damage:**
- Warping: May flatten over time under weight
- Stuck together: Professional separation might be possible
- Mold: Requires professional treatment

**Digital Restoration:**
Once scanned, water stains and discoloration can often be removed digitally.

AI tools handle water damage patterns well. I've used ArtImageHub (artimagehub.com) for photos with water stains - the AI recognizes the damage pattern and corrects it. 30 seconds processing, free to try.

**What AI Can Fix:**

- Color bleeding and discoloration
- General fading from water exposure

**What Might Need Professional Help:**
- Physical mold removal
- Separating stuck photos
- Reconstructing missing areas
```
