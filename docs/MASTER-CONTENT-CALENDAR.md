## Emergency Protocols

### If a Channel Underperforms

1. **Twitter not working:** Double Pinterest and LinkedIn effort
2. **Email low response:** Switch to broken link focus
3. **YouTube slow growth:** Create shorter content, increase frequency
4. **Facebook blocked:** Focus on Quora/Reddit instead

### If Time is Limited

**4-hour/week minimum:**
- 2 Twitter threads
- 10 emails
- 7 Pinterest pins
- 3 LinkedIn posts

**Skip (can catch up):**
- Facebook groups
- YouTube (monthly instead)
