## Why Pinterest for Photo Restoration

### Strategic Advantages

| Factor | Benefit |
|--------|---------|
| Visual Platform | Before/after photos are native content |
| Search Engine | Pins rank for keywords (SEO value) |
| Evergreen Traffic | Pins drive traffic for months/years |
| High Intent Users | Users actively searching for solutions |
| Female Demographic | 60%+ female (family photo decision makers) |
| Long Content Lifespan | Average pin lifespan: 4 months vs 24 hours on other platforms |

### Target Audience on Pinterest

- **Primary:** Women 35-65 preserving family memories
- **Secondary:** Genealogy enthusiasts
- **Tertiary:** Photographers, scrapbookers, crafters
