## Production Notes

### Equipment Needed
- **Screen recording:** QuickTime (Mac), OBS Studio (free), or Loom
- **Video editing:** CapCut (free, easy), DaVinci Resolve (free, pro), iMovie (Mac)
- **Voiceover:** MacBook microphone (acceptable), Blue Yeti (better), or AI voice (ElevenLabs)
- **Music:** Epidemic Sound, Artlist, or royalty-free (YouTube Audio Library)

### Recording Checklist
- [ ] Clear browser cache/cookies (clean demo)
- [ ] Hide bookmark bar and extensions
- [ ] Set browser to 1920px width or full screen
- [ ] Prepare before/after images in advance
- [ ] Record at 60fps for smooth slow-motion
- [ ] Record 10-20% longer than needed (trim in edit)
- [ ] Record multiple takes of key moments

### Editing Tips
- **Pace:** Keep it fast. 2-3 seconds per scene max (except results reveal)
- **Music:** Upbeat, modern, not too loud. Fade under voiceover.
- **Transitions:** Simple cuts or quick fades. No cheesy wipes.
- **Text:** Large, bold, high contrast. Inter or Montserrat font.
- **Colors:** Match brand (#0071e3 blue, purple gradient)
- **Captions:** Add subtitles for accessibility and silent viewing

### Platform-Specific Formats

**Product Hunt (recommended):**
- **Format:** 16:9 landscape
- **Duration:** 30-60 seconds
- **File size:** <100MB
- **Format:** MP4, H.264

**Instagram/TikTok (bonus):**
- **Format:** 9:16 vertical OR 1:1 square
- **Duration:** 15-30 seconds
- **Style:** Fast cuts, text overlays, trending music
- **Hook:** First 3 seconds critical

**YouTube (extended version):**
- **Format:** 16:9 landscape
- **Duration:** 60-90 seconds
- **Style:** More narrative, slower pace
- **Include:** Channel subscribe CTA
