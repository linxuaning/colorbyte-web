Sam's Club (the Walmart membership warehouse) offers photo printing services that compare favorably to Costco in quality and price. Like other photo printing services, Sam's Club prints what you provide — it doesn't restore photographs. Here's what to know.

---

## What Sam's Club Photo Offers

**In-store (Photo Center):**
- Standard prints: 4×6 through large format
- Same-day pickup at many locations
- Canvas prints, poster prints
- Basic editing tools in-store

**Online (samsclub.com/photos):**
- Photo books, canvas wraps, wall art
- Standard prints with in-store or ship delivery
- Competitive pricing on bulk orders

**Sam's Club Photo quality:** Generally excellent — comparable to Costco and significantly better than CVS/Walgreens pharmacy kiosks. Sam's Club uses professional photo printers with calibrated color and quality paper.

## The Restoration Workflow for Sam's Club

**Step 1: Digitize**
Scan at 600+ DPI. Library scanners are free. Sam's Club photo center does not offer scanning of physical prints.

**Step 2: Restore**
Upload to ArtImageHub — $4.99, 30–90 seconds. Download HD restored file.

**Step 3: Print at Sam's Club**
Upload online at samsclub.com/photos → select size and paper type → choose in-store pickup or delivery.

**For large format prints (16×20, 20×24, canvas):** Sam's Club's printing quality makes it particularly good for display-quality prints of restored family portraits.

## Why Print Quality Matters for Restored Photos

AI restoration produces HD restored files with significant quality improvement over the original scan. To see this improvement in print:

- Low-quality printers (CVS, Walgreens) print at acceptable quality for 4×6 but compress color and detail
- Sam's Club and Costco print at calibrated professional quality — the restored detail in faces, color accuracy after fading correction, and overall clarity are visible in print

For a portrait you're framing and displaying, the difference between pharmacy kiosk and professional print quality is visible. Sam's Club and Costco are the right choice for restored family portrait prints intended for display.
