# Restoring Little League and Youth Sports Team Photos

Youth sports team photos were an annual childhood ritual — the formal team lineup, the individual portrait with the bat or ball, the coach with his arm around the championship team. These images document childhood friendships and the communities formed around youth athletics.

## The Challenge

Team portrait photography used specific printing technologies that aged predictably — the Kodacolor prints of the 1970s fade toward cyan, while the Fujicolor prints of the 1980s shift toward magenta. Knowing the printing technology allows for targeted restoration.

## How AI Helps

AI restoration databases include color shift profiles for major consumer printing technologies by decade, allowing the algorithm to apply technically accurate color correction rather than generic enhancement. This produces results that accurately represent the original colors.
