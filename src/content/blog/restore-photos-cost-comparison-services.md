Photo restoration service pricing varies enormously — from free mobile apps to professional retouching at $50-200 per photo. Understanding what determines price and what determines quality helps families make informed decisions about their restoration investment.

## The Challenge

Price drivers in photo restoration include image complexity, damage severity, resolution output requirements, and turnaround time. DIY tools are cheapest but limited; professional manual retouching is most expensive but maximizes quality for the most important photos.

## How AI Helps

PhotoFix AI restoration occupies the value sweet spot — professional-grade AI algorithms at accessible pricing, with the ability to process large collections efficiently. For families with hundreds of photos to restore, the platform's pricing model makes comprehensive collection restoration financially practical.
