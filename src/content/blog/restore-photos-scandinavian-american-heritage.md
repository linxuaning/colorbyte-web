# Preserving Scandinavian American Heritage and Community Photos

Scandinavian American community photos document the heritage of Norwegian, Swedish, Danish, and Finnish immigrants who built farming and fishing communities across the upper Midwest and Pacific Northwest. Lutheran church life, Midsommar celebrations, and ethnic mutual aid societies created rich photographic records.

## The Challenge

Upper Midwest storage conditions — extreme cold in winter and heat in summer — created particular deterioration challenges for Scandinavian American photo collections. Temperature cycling caused print emulsion cracking and curl that physical handling worsened over decades.

## How AI Helps

AI restoration handles emulsion crack damage using inpainting algorithms that reconstruct image content across crack lines. The craquelure pattern of cold-climate cracking is distinctively different from other damage types, and the restoration algorithm applies crack-specific treatment.
