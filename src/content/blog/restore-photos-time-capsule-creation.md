# Creating a Family Photo Time Capsule for Future Generations

A family photo time capsule — designed to be opened by great-grandchildren and beyond — requires the most archival-quality preservation approach. The images must survive another 100 years and remain accessible on technologies not yet invented.

## The Challenge

Time capsule photography preservation requires format choices, storage media, and documentation standards designed for century-scale survival. Open digital formats, multiple storage copies in different locations, and comprehensive metadata documentation are all essential.

## How AI Helps

AI restoration for time capsule archives creates the highest-quality base files suitable for century-scale preservation — full-resolution TIFF files with comprehensive embedded metadata, detailed restoration documentation, and human-readable companion files that allow the archive to be understood without specialized software.

## Next Steps

Gather your photographs, scan at the highest resolution available (600 DPI minimum), and visit [PhotoFix](/old-photo-restoration) to start the restoration process. AI tools can handle damage that once required expensive professional services, making preservation accessible for every family.
