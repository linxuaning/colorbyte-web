# Preserving Grandfather Military Portrait and Service Photos

Grandfather military portraits are among the most formally preserved family photos — the dress uniform portrait before deployment, the unit photo with fellow soldiers, the discharge photo that ended service. These images of men in uniform document both personal service and national history.

## The Challenge

Military portrait photography used studio-quality equipment and professional-grade printing, producing photos with better initial quality than typical consumer snapshots. However, even professional-grade prints deteriorate over decades without archival storage.

## How AI Helps

AI restoration trained on military portrait photography recovers the sharp detail of uniform insignia, ribbons, and medals that identify service branch, rank, and campaigns. This information transforms a faded portrait into a documented service record.

## Next Steps

Gather your photographs, scan at the highest resolution available (600 DPI minimum), and visit [PhotoFix](/old-photo-restoration) to start the restoration process. AI tools can handle damage that once required expensive professional services, making preservation accessible for every family.
