# Preserving Immigrant Passport and Identity Document Photos

Immigrant passport and identity photos are the smallest but most significant images in family collections — the formal portrait taken before departure, the Ellis Island documentation photo, the naturalization certificate image. These tiny photographs carry enormous historical weight.

## The Challenge

Document photos are among the most damaged in typical family collections because they were handled constantly, carried in wallets, and subjected to official processing that involved rubber stamps, adhesives, and bureaucratic handling over years.

## How AI Helps

AI restoration specializes in recovering small-format document photos, which require different enhancement approaches than larger prints. The algorithm enhances facial detail at document photo scale while removing adhesive residue, stamp impressions, and handling damage.

## Next Steps

Gather your photographs, scan at the highest resolution available (600 DPI minimum), and visit [restore old photos online](/old-photo-restoration) to start the restoration process. AI tools can handle damage that once required expensive professional services, making preservation accessible for every family.
