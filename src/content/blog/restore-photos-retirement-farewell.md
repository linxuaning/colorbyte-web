# Preserving Retirement Party and Farewell Photos

Retirement photos document the close of career chapters — the workplace gathered to honor someone who devoted decades of service, the gold watch presentation, the speech that tried to capture a career in five minutes. These workplace milestone photos capture professional identity at its most honored moment.

## The Challenge

Workplace and office party photography from the 1970s-1990s was typically casual snapshot photography in office environments — fluorescent lighting, cluttered backgrounds, and the informal nature of workplace social events created technically challenging photos.

## How AI Helps

AI restoration trained on indoor office and workplace photography handles the specific challenges of fluorescent lighting, office clutter, and informal social event photography. The restoration recovers faces and celebratory moments from technically imperfect workplace photos.

## Next Steps

Gather your photographs, scan at the highest resolution available (600 DPI minimum), and visit [PhotoFix](/old-photo-restoration) to start the restoration process. AI tools can handle damage that once required expensive professional services, making preservation accessible for every family.
