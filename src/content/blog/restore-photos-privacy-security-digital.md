Digital family photo archives contain sensitive personal information — faces, locations, dates, and family relationships that require privacy consideration. As photos move from private boxes to digital formats, understanding privacy best practices protects family information.

## The Challenge

Photo privacy concerns include facial recognition by third-party platforms, metadata exposure in uploaded files, cloud storage vulnerability to breaches, and the permanence of shared digital images. Each concern has practical management approaches.

## How AI Helps

PhotoFix privacy commitment includes not using uploaded family photos for training data without explicit consent, providing EXIF data stripping for privacy-sensitive sharing, and offering private archive options that don't expose photos to public search or indexing.

## Next Steps

Gather your photographs, scan at the highest resolution available (600 DPI minimum), and visit [PhotoFix](/old-photo-restoration) to start the restoration process. AI tools can handle damage that once required expensive professional services, making preservation accessible for every family.
