Beach vacation photos from the 1960s and 1970s document the golden age of American family tourism — station wagons loaded with luggage, motel swimming pools, and the joy of children discovering the ocean. These images capture American optimism at its most vivid.

## The Challenge

Beach environment photography presented severe challenges for early color film — the UV intensity at beach locations accelerated silver oxidation, while salt air physically attacked print surfaces. The bright high-key lighting of beach environments also caused systematic overexposure.

## How AI Helps

AI beach photography restoration incorporates specialized handling for salt air physical damage and UV-induced silver oxidation. Sky and water blue recovery uses period-accurate color profile correction to restore the saturated summer blues of beach vacation photos.
