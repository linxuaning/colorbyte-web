Military academy and ROTC photos document the formation of military officers — the rigorous training, the formal reviews, the graduation ceremonies that marked the beginning of service. These photos connect families to traditions of military service spanning generations.

## The Challenge

Military institution photography was typically professional in quality, using service photographers who understood the requirements of documenting military uniforms and ceremonies. However, institutional archives frequently suffered from poor storage conditions after original documentation purposes were served.

## How AI Helps

AI restoration recovers military academy photos from institutional archive storage conditions, which often involved non-archival binders and storage rooms without climate control. Detail enhancement recovers uniform insignia and unit identifications crucial for military history research.

## Next Steps

Gather your photographs, scan at the highest resolution available (600 DPI minimum), and visit [PhotoFix](/old-photo-restoration) to start the restoration process. AI tools can handle damage that once required expensive professional services, making preservation accessible for every family.
