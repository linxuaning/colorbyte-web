Pre-immigration photographs are the most precious family images for many immigrant families — the only visual record of the place and people left behind when families made the journey to America. For many families, these are the only photos that exist of the homeland they will never return to.

## The Challenge

Pre-immigration photos arrived in America having survived the journey themselves — carried in luggage, hidden in clothing, mailed across oceans. The physical damage of transatlantic travel adds to whatever deterioration occurred in storage both before and after the journey.

## How AI Helps

AI restoration for pre-immigration photos applies maximum recovery protocols, treating each image as potentially the only surviving visual record of an irreplaceable place and moment in family history.

## Next Steps

Gather your photographs, scan at the highest resolution available (600 DPI minimum), and visit [PhotoFix](/old-photo-restoration) to start the restoration process. AI tools can handle damage that once required expensive professional services, making preservation accessible for every family.
