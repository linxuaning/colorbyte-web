High school graduation photos span nearly every decade of modern photography. Each era's graduates wear different styles, pose in different ways, and are captured by the photographic technology of their time. These milestone images mark the beginning of adult life.

## The Challenge

Graduation photos represent a cross-section of photographic technologies — 1940s formal portraits, 1960s color prints, 1970s school photo packages, 1990s digital previews on film. Each technology has its own deterioration pattern and restoration requirements.

## How AI Helps

AI restoration automatically identifies the photographic technology used for each image and applies the appropriate restoration protocol. This means a 1955 graduation portrait and a 1985 school photo package print receive fundamentally different treatments.

## Next Steps

Gather your photographs, scan at the highest resolution available (600 DPI minimum), and visit [PhotoFix](/old-photo-restoration) to start the restoration process. AI tools can handle damage that once required expensive professional services, making preservation accessible for every family.
