# Restored Photos as Gifts for Grandparents' Milestone Birthdays

A grandparent's 80th or 90th birthday creates the perfect occasion to restore and return family photos — the wedding portrait from 60 years ago, the photos of the grandparent as a young parent, the images from decades of family life that time has dimmed.

## The Challenge

Milestone birthday gift restoration involves coordination with other family members to gather the most meaningful photos, organize surprise presentation, and ensure the restored images tell the story of the grandparent's complete life.
