# Using Restored Photos to Date Family History

Dating old family photos without written information requires visual clues that restoration can reveal — clothing styles, hairstyles, photographic formats, studio mark designs, and background details all help researchers narrow down when a photo was taken.

## The Challenge

Restoration enhances the visibility of dating clues that deterioration obscured — the text on a studio backdrop stand, the pattern of a wallpaper background, the insignia on a uniform, or the model year details of an automobile visible through a window.

## How AI Helps

AI restoration trained on historical photography actively prioritizes the recovery of dating clues. The enhanced details feed into AI-powered dating analysis tools that can narrow the date range of an unknown photo to within 5-10 years based on multiple visual evidence factors.
