# Restoring Garage and Car Restoration Heritage Photos

Garage and car restoration photos document a quintessentially American passion — the project car, the customized hot rod, the father and son working together under the hood. These images capture mechanical skill and the transmission of automotive knowledge.

## The Challenge

Garage photography faced extreme contrast challenges — the dark interior with a bright open door, the mix of work lamps and natural light, the dark under-hood environment. Consumer cameras consistently failed to capture both the brightly lit exterior and the dark work space.

## How AI Helps

AI HDR reconstruction trained on automotive workshop photography recovers both exterior car detail and dark interior workshop environments simultaneously. The algorithm handles the extreme contrast typical of garage photography, recovering the full scene.

## Next Steps

Gather your photographs, scan at the highest resolution available (600 DPI minimum), and visit [PhotoFix](/old-photo-restoration) to start the restoration process. AI tools can handle damage that once required expensive professional services, making preservation accessible for every family.
