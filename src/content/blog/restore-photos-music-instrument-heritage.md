Family music photos document the musical heritage passed across generations — the grandmother at her piano, the grandfather's fiddle, the father's guitar, the child's first violin lesson. These images carry cultural tradition as surely as the music itself.

## The Challenge

Musical instrument photography presents specific challenges — the reflective surfaces of piano finishes, brass instruments, and guitar lacquer created blown-out highlights that obliterated detail. Indoor performance lighting created the same mixed-source challenges as church and theater photography.

## How AI Helps

AI specular highlight recovery algorithms recover instrument detail from overexposed reflective surfaces by modeling the instrument geometry and reconstructing lost highlight information. Combined with indoor lighting correction, these tools transform previously unusable music photos.

## Next Steps

Gather your photographs, scan at the highest resolution available (600 DPI minimum), and visit [PhotoFix](/old-photo-restoration) to start the restoration process. AI tools can handle damage that once required expensive professional services, making preservation accessible for every family.
