# Preserving Irish American Heritage and Community Photos

Irish American community photos document the heritage of families who fled famine and discrimination to build new lives in America. St. Patrick's Day parades, Gaelic music sessions, and GAA sports events created photographic records of cultural persistence.

## The Challenge

Irish American photo collections often span the longest time periods among immigrant communities — families with pre-Civil War Irish roots have photographic collections from the daguerreotype era forward. These collections require the widest range of restoration technologies.

## How AI Helps

AI restoration platform supports 15+ photographic technologies from daguerreotypes to digital prints, allowing a single Irish American family collection spanning 150+ years to be processed in a unified workflow with era-appropriate treatment for each image.

## Next Steps

Gather your photographs, scan at the highest resolution available (600 DPI minimum), and visit [restore old photos online](/old-photo-restoration) to start the restoration process. AI tools can handle damage that once required expensive professional services, making preservation accessible for every family.
