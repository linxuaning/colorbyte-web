From the lobstermen of Maine to the shrimpers of the Gulf Coast, fishing families have documented their lives in photographs for over a century. These images of boats, nets, docks, and the sea connect generations to their maritime roots.

## The Challenge

Salt air and marine environments are particularly destructive to photographic materials. Silver halide prints experience accelerated tarnishing in coastal environments. Boathouse storage exposed photos to constant humidity fluctuation.

## How AI Helps

AI tools specifically address silver mirroring — the metallic sheen that obscures image details in tarnished prints. By modeling the underlying image through the tarnish layer, restoration software recovers faces and details that appeared lost.

## Next Steps

Gather your photographs, scan at the highest resolution available (600 DPI minimum), and visit [PhotoFix](/old-photo-restoration) to start the restoration process. AI tools can handle damage that once required expensive professional services, making preservation accessible for every family.
