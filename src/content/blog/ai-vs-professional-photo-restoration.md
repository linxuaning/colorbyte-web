## Frequently Asked Questions

**Is AI restoration as good as professional work?**

Short answer: For most photos, AI produces excellent results comparable to basic professional restoration. Professionals still excel at severe damage and artistic reconstruction. Try AI first—you may be surprised by the quality.

**Can I use AI restoration and then hire a professional if needed?**

Short answer: Absolutely—this is a smart approach. AI restoration can handle 80-90% of most collections. Save professional services (and costs) for truly challenging photos that need expert attention.

**Will AI restoration damage my original photo?**

Short answer: No, AI restoration is completely non-destructive. It works on digital copies only. Your original photo is never altered. This is actually safer than some professional physical restoration techniques.

**How do I know if my photo needs professional restoration?**

Short answer: Try AI first. If the result isn't satisfactory—particularly for photos with large missing sections, severe water damage, or faces that need reconstruction—consider professional help.
