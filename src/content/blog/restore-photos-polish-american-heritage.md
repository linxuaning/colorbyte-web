Polish American community photos document the vibrant cultural life of Polish immigrants and their descendants — Pulaski Day parades, Dyngus Day celebrations, Polish National Alliance events, and the polka music tradition that united communities from Chicago to Buffalo.

## The Challenge

Polish American immigrant photos frequently show the physical damage of transatlantic immigration — photographs were among the few possessions immigrants carried, and they suffered accordingly from the conditions of steerage travel.

## How AI Helps

AI restoration addresses the compound physical and chemical damage characteristic of immigration-survivor photos. The algorithm prioritizes recovering legible faces from the human subjects, which are typically the most important content for family history purposes.

## Next Steps

Gather your photographs, scan at the highest resolution available (600 DPI minimum), and visit [PhotoFix](/old-photo-restoration) to start the restoration process. AI tools can handle damage that once required expensive professional services, making preservation accessible for every family.
