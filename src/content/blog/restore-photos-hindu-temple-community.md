Hindu temple and South Asian American community photos document the establishment of cultural institutions and the maintenance of religious heritage in America. Diwali celebrations, temple consecrations, and cultural festivals created photographic records that connect generations.

## The Challenge

Diwali and temple ceremony photos present extreme lighting challenges — oil lamp and diya illumination creates warm low-key lighting that consumer cameras rendered with limited accuracy. Traditional costume colors, particularly the saturated reds and oranges of festival dress, were often lost to fading.

## How AI Helps

AI restoration trained on Indian festival photography accurately recovers the vibrant colors of traditional costume and ceremony decoration. Specialized warm-light correction addresses the distinctive quality of oil lamp illumination.

## Next Steps

Gather your photographs, scan at the highest resolution available (600 DPI minimum), and visit [PhotoFix](/old-photo-restoration) to start the restoration process. AI tools can handle damage that once required expensive professional services, making preservation accessible for every family.
