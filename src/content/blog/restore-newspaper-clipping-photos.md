## Newspaper Photo Challenges

### Halftone Patterns

Newspapers print using dot patterns that create visual noise when scanned. Modern AI can help reduce this pattern while preserving image detail.

### Paper Deterioration

**Common Issues:**
- Severe yellowing
- Brittle, torn edges
- Foxing spots
- Ink bleeding

## Best Results from Newsprint

### Scanning Tips

- Scan at 600+ DPI
- Use descreen feature if available
- Keep clipping flat
- Clean scanner glass
