**Will AI enhancement make the face look like a different person?**

Short answer: Quality AI enhancement should not change identity—it enhances existing features, not invents new ones. Our tool is designed to clarify and upscale while maintaining the authentic appearance of the photographed person. The result should look like a clearer version of the same person.

**Can I create a print-quality portrait from a small face in a group photo?**

Short answer: Depending on source size and quality, yes. Faces 50+ pixels in the original often produce excellent results suitable for moderate-sized prints. **How do I get the best results when cropping a small face?**

Short answer: Include generous margins around the face—hair, neck, shoulders, and some background. Don't crop too tightly. Start with the highest resolution scan possible. The AI uses context to enhance faces, so more surrounding information helps. Avoid cropping at odd angles.

**Can group photos from different eras all be enhanced the same way?**

Short answer: Yes, our AI handles photos from all eras—from 1800s to recent digital. The technology adapts to different image characteristics including sepia tones, black and white, and color. Each era has typical characteristics the AI recognizes and handles appropriately.
