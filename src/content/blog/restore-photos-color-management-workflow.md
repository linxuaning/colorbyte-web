# Color Management for Family Photo Restoration

Color management ensures that restored photos look consistent across different displays, printers, and sharing platforms. Without proper color management, a photo that looks perfect on one screen may appear too warm on another or too dark when printed.

## The Challenge

Color management involves display calibration, embedded color profiles in image files, and color-managed workflows throughout the scanning, restoration, and output process. Each step affects the final color accuracy of restored photos.

## How AI Helps

ArtImageHub processes all restored photos with sRGB and AdobeRGB color profile options, providing files suitable for both web display and professional printing. The platform's color management workflow ensures accurate color representation across the most common display and printing environments.
