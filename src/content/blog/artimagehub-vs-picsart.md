Differences between products are mostly pricing model and workflow, not raw AI quality.


Picsart is one of the most popular creative photo editing platforms — over 150 million monthly active users across mobile and web. ArtImageHub is a specialized AI pipeline for old photo restoration. Both handle photos; they serve very different purposes.

## What Picsart Offers

Picsart is a **creative editing platform** with a broad feature set:

**Photo editing:** Filters, effects, adjustments, text, stickers, frames

**AI tools:**
- AI Enhance: general image enhancement and upscaling
- AI Background Removal
- AI Replace (generative fill)
- AI Image Generator
- AI Avatar

**Creative tools:** Collage maker, design templates, video editing

**Social component:** Built-in community for sharing edited content

Picsart is primarily a creative expression tool — strong for social media content, artistic photo editing, and visual design.

---

## What Picsart Cannot Do for Old Photos

**No historical face reconstruction:** Picsart's AI Enhance applies general sharpening and upscaling — not CodeFormer's specialized historical face reconstruction. The difference is visible on 1940s–1960s portraits where faces have lost significant detail.

**No systematic fading correction:** Picsart's brightness and contrast tools are general adjustments. GFPGAN's systematic correction of historical photographic paper yellowing is a trained, specific correction — not general brightness.

**No scratch pattern removal:** Picsart's tools don't include healing brush or clone stamp equivalent for removing physical damage from old prints.

**Subscription model:** Picsart Gold is $13/month or $55/year for full AI tool access.

## When Picsart Makes Sense

**Modern photo editing:** Picsart excels at creative editing for social media and casual photography.
