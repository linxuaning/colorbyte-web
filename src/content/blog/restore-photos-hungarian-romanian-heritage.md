# Preserving Hungarian and Romanian American Heritage Photos

Hungarian and Romanian American photos document the Eastern European immigrant experience in America — the Hungarian Reformed Church, Romanian Orthodox community celebrations, and the cultural organizations that maintained distinct national identities within the American mosaic.

## The Challenge

Eastern European immigrant photo collections share common preservation challenges — many photos were damaged during multiple displacement events, including wartime refugee experiences and multiple migration stages before final settlement in America.

## How AI Helps

AI restoration addresses the layered damage of multiply-displaced photo collections. The systematic damage analysis identifies each damage type and layer, applying treatments from most severe to most subtle, recovering maximum content from each image.
