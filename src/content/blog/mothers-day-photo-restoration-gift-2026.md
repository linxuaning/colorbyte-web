## What AI Photo Restoration Can Fix

- **Scratches and tears:** Physical damage from decades of handling
- **Fading:** Chemical degradation that turns prints yellow or drains color
- **Blurry faces:** Old cameras often had soft focus; AI reconstructs face detail
- **Water damage:** Staining and tide marks from floods or damp storage
- **Low contrast:** Flat, washed-out tones restored to natural-looking depth
