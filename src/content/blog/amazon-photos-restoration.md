Amazon Photos is Amazon's cloud photo storage service — included free with Amazon Prime membership. Many people store their digitized family photos here. Here's what Amazon Photos does and doesn't do for old, damaged family photographs.

---

## What Amazon Photos Offers

**For Prime members (free with membership):**
- Unlimited full-resolution photo storage
- 5 GB video storage
- Family Vault: share storage with up to 5 family members
- Automatic backup from phone and computer
- Basic organization: albums, search, date sorting

**Amazon Prints:**
- Photo prints (4×6 through larger formats) ordered directly from Amazon Photos
- Photo books, canvas prints, wall art
- Amazon fulfillment: shipped with Prime delivery benefits

---

## Does Amazon Photos Restore Old Photos?

**No.** Amazon Photos is a storage and printing service. It stores photos you upload and prints them at the quality you provide. There are no restoration, enhancement, or AI correction features in Amazon Photos.

If you upload a faded, scratched 1955 family portrait, Amazon Photos stores it exactly as uploaded. If you print it, Amazon prints the faded, scratched version.

---

## The Amazon Photos Workflow for Old Photo Restoration

**Step 1: Scan old prints** (600+ DPI, library scanner or Google PhotoScan on phone)

**Step 2: Upload to Amazon Photos** for cloud storage and backup (free with Prime — unlimited full-resolution storage means your scans are safely backed up)

**Step 3: Download the scan to your device** or open it from Amazon Photos

**Step 4: Upload to ArtImageHub** ($4.99, 30–90 seconds) for AI restoration

**Step 5: Upload the restored version back to Amazon Photos** (for permanent cloud storage of the restored file)

**Step 6: Print via Amazon Prints** if desired — the restored version prints with full quality

## Why Amazon Photos Is Good for Family Photo Archives

**Unlimited full-resolution storage (Prime):** Unlike Google Photos (which compresses for free storage) or iCloud (which has storage limits), Amazon Photos stores unlimited full-resolution photos for Prime members. This makes it ideal for archiving both original scans and restored versions of family photographs.

**Family Vault:** Share the archive with up to 5 family members — all can access and download the restored photos.

**Amazon Prints quality:** Amazon Prints quality is comparable to Costco and Shutterfly — professional-grade for standard print sizes.
