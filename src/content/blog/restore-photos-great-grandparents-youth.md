One of the most powerful experiences in family photo restoration is seeing great-grandparents as young adults — the 25-year-old in her wedding dress, the 19-year-old soldier before deployment, the young couple just starting out. These images create connections that abstractions about family history cannot.

## The Challenge

Very old formal portraits were taken with the intent of creating a lasting record, often with the best available photographic technology of the era. However, even carefully taken portraits from 80-100 years ago show significant deterioration that obscures the youth and vitality of the subjects.

## How AI Helps

AI restoration specifically optimizes for recovering the vitality and detail of faces in formal portraits. The algorithm balances skin tone accuracy with detail recovery, ensuring that great-grandparents' faces are rendered with both technical accuracy and the human warmth of the original subjects.

## Next Steps

Gather your photographs, scan at the highest resolution available (600 DPI minimum), and visit [PhotoFix](/old-photo-restoration) to start the restoration process. AI tools can handle damage that once required expensive professional services, making preservation accessible for every family.
