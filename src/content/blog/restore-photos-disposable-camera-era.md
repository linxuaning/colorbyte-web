# Preserving Disposable Camera Era Photos from the 1990s

Disposable cameras democratized photography in the 1990s — tossed into purses for concerts, passed around at parties, and given to children for their own perspective. These informal cameras captured unguarded moments that more formal photography missed.

## The Challenge

Disposable camera prints are among the most technically limited photos in typical family collections — the fixed-focus lens, the limited-range flash, and the consumer-grade film and processing created prints with characteristic softness, grain, and color inaccuracy.

## How AI Helps

AI enhancement specifically developed for consumer snapshot photography dramatically improves disposable camera print quality. The combination of denoising, sharpening, and color correction transforms these casual 1990s snapshots into archival-quality family photos.
