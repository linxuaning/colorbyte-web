## What Makes a Good Restoration Gift

The best photo restoration gift targets the photo she cares most about but has done nothing with because of damage.

Signs you've found the right photo:
- She's mentioned it specifically ("I wish this photo weren't so damaged")
- It's of someone she loved who's no longer alive
- It's the only photo of its kind—her as a child, her parents young, her own early relationship
- It's been kept despite being too damaged to display

The more meaningful the photo and the more significant the damage, the more powerful the restoration. A photo that was "too bad to do anything with" transformed into something frameable is a dramatically better gift than a moderately improved photo that was already decent.

There's a range of photo-based gift options. Here's how they compare:
