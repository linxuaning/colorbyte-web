Environmental movement photos document the organizing that created the EPA, the Clean Air Act, and the Endangered Species Act — the first Earth Day in 1970, the wilderness campaigns, and the community activists who fought industrial pollution in their backyards.

## The Challenge

Environmental movement photos were stored in the informal conditions of underfunded activist organizations — file cabinet storage, garage shelving, and basement filing systems that prioritized access over preservation.

## How AI Helps

AI restoration makes environmental movement archives accessible by recovering photos from typical activist organization storage conditions. The result is an enhanced documentary record of the organizing that shaped American environmental policy.

## Next Steps

Gather your photographs, scan at the highest resolution available (600 DPI minimum), and visit [PhotoFix](/old-photo-restoration) to start the restoration process. AI tools can handle damage that once required expensive professional services, making preservation accessible for every family.
