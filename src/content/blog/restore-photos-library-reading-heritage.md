Photos of families reading — the child at story time, the grandmother reading to grandchildren, the scholar in the library — document the transmission of intellectual culture across generations. These quiet images capture values as clearly as any formal portrait.

## The Challenge

Library and reading photos share the mixed lighting challenges of most institutional indoor photography. Public library lighting from the 1950s-1980s was typically a combination of natural window light and early fluorescent fixtures that created complex color casts.

## How AI Helps

AI multi-source lighting correction trained on institutional photography environments addresses the complex mixed lighting of library settings. The algorithm simultaneously corrects for natural, fluorescent, and incandescent light sources present in different areas of the same image.

## Next Steps

Gather your photographs, scan at the highest resolution available (600 DPI minimum), and visit [PhotoFix](/old-photo-restoration) to start the restoration process. AI tools can handle damage that once required expensive professional services, making preservation accessible for every family.
