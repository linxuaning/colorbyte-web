# Restoring Old Photos for Genealogy Research

Genealogy research transformed by the discovery of an old family photo — but only if the photo contains legible details. Faces, clothing, background details, and text within photos all provide genealogical evidence that researchers use to connect family lines.

## The Challenge

Genealogy-focused restoration prioritizes different elements than casual preservation — the researchers need readable text in backgrounds (store signs, ship names, uniform insignia), identifying details in faces for comparison with other family photos, and contextual details that help date and locate images.

## How AI Helps

AI genealogy enhancement applies targeted restoration to the specific areas of highest research value — text regions receive aggressive sharpening, faces receive detail enhancement for comparison purposes, and background context elements receive enhancement to reveal location and date clues.
