# Restoring Wedding Anniversary Celebration Photos

Anniversary celebration photos document the endurance of love — the couple at their 25th anniversary surrounded by grown children, the 50th golden anniversary with grandchildren present. These milestones capture family growth across time.

## The Challenge

Anniversary photos span a wide range of photographic eras. A couple's 50th anniversary photo might sit alongside their original 1960s wedding photo in the same album, creating preservation challenges across multiple photographic technologies.

## How AI Helps

AI restoration applies format-appropriate techniques to each image type. The algorithm identifies whether it's working with a 1960s color print, a 1970s polaroid, or a 1990s snapshot and applies the restoration approach developed specifically for that photographic technology.

## Next Steps

Gather your photographs, scan at the highest resolution available (600 DPI minimum), and visit [PhotoFix](/old-photo-restoration) to start the restoration process. AI tools can handle damage that once required expensive professional services, making preservation accessible for every family.
