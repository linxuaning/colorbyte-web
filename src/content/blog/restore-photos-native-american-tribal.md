# Preserving Native American Tribal and Family Heritage Photos

Native American tribal and family photos document the survival and continuation of indigenous cultures despite centuries of attempted erasure. Photos from tribal ceremonies, boarding school eras, and contemporary powwows are crucial documents of cultural persistence.

## The Challenge

Native American photo collections include particularly early photographic images — the 19th-century anthropological photographs taken by outsiders, as well as family snapshots taken by community members. Both types require specialized restoration approaches.

## How AI Helps

AI restoration applies culturally sensitive recovery protocols to Native American photos. The algorithm treats both early anthropological photographs and family snapshots with equal care, restoring the human dignity and cultural specificity that historical photography sometimes failed to capture.
