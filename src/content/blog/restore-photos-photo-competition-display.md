Restored family photos entered in genealogical society exhibitions, local historical society displays, or photo heritage competitions require the highest level of technical quality. These public presentations represent both personal family heritage and collective community history.

## The Challenge

Exhibition-quality restoration requires specific technical standards — resolution sufficient for large-format display, color accuracy appropriate for archival reproduction, and preservation of the historical authenticity that makes heritage photos valuable as documentary images.

## How AI Helps

PhotoFix exhibition-quality processing produces files meeting museum archival standards — full-resolution TIFF delivery with ICC color profiles appropriate for fine-art printing, complete with restoration documentation suitable for exhibition caption text.

## Next Steps

Gather your photographs, scan at the highest resolution available (600 DPI minimum), and visit [PhotoFix](/old-photo-restoration) to start the restoration process. AI tools can handle damage that once required expensive professional services, making preservation accessible for every family.
