# Preserving School Play and Performance Memories

School plays and recitals created memories that last lifetimes — the nervous child in costume, the proud parent in the audience, the curtain call. These performance photos capture a side of children rarely seen in everyday snapshots.

## The Challenge

Performance photos face distinctive challenges: stage lighting created uneven exposure, costumes with metallic elements overloaded early color film, and photos taken from auditorium distances suffered from camera shake at slow shutter speeds.

## How AI Helps

AI deblurring technology was developed specifically to address camera shake in low-light performance photography. Combined with local exposure correction, these tools recover crisp details from previously blurry stage photos.

## Next Steps

Gather your photographs, scan at the highest resolution available (600 DPI minimum), and visit [restore old photos online](/old-photo-restoration) to start the restoration process. AI tools can handle damage that once required expensive professional services, making preservation accessible for every family.
