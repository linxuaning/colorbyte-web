# Preserving Cartes de Visite Photo Collections

Cartes de visite were the business cards of Victorian social life — small portrait photos that people exchanged, collected, and pasted into special albums. A Victorian family album might contain cartes de visite of family, friends, celebrities, and political figures.

## The Challenge

Cartes de visite collections present batch restoration challenges — albums typically contain dozens of photos with varied condition and multiple photographic technologies. Each image needs individual assessment while maintaining consistent quality across the collection.

## How AI Helps

AI batch restoration efficiently processes entire cartes de visite collections, applying individualized treatment to each image while ensuring the collection as a whole achieves consistent visual quality. The result is a complete Victorian family social network in restored digital form.
