# Identifying Families in Unknown and Orphaned Old Photos

Every antique store and estate sale has boxes of old photos without names or dates — orphaned images of unknown families. For descendants who discover unlabeled family photos, restoration and enhancement can reveal identifying details that reconnect these images with family history.

## The Challenge

Unknown photo identification benefits from every detail in an image — clothing dates, background landmarks, military insignia, studio names on cabinet card backs, and the distinctive features of faces that might match known family members.

## How AI Helps

AI restoration for identification purposes maximizes detail recovery in all potential identifying elements — background text, clothing details, facial features, and studio information. The enhanced image gives researchers maximum evidence for identification efforts.

## Next Steps

Gather your photographs, scan at the highest resolution available (600 DPI minimum), and visit [old photo restoration tool](/old-photo-restoration) to start the restoration process.
