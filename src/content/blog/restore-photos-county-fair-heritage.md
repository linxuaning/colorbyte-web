# Restoring County Fair and State Fair Family Photos

County and state fairs have been gathering places for American families for over 150 years. Those photos of prize-winning livestock, cotton candy, and Ferris wheel rides capture a rural heritage that shaped generations.

## The Challenge

Fair photos often suffer from bright outdoor light causing overexposure, or from being taken at night with early flash technology that created harsh shadows and red-eye effects. Many are also water-stained from being stored in barn lofts or outbuildings.

## How AI Helps

AI restoration handles both overexposed and underexposed areas simultaneously, recovering detail from blown-out skies and dark shadows. Color correction algorithms restore the vibrant hues of carnival lights and summer sunshine.

## Next Steps

Gather your photographs, scan at the highest resolution available (600 DPI minimum), and visit [AI photo restoration tool](/old-photo-restoration) to start the restoration process. AI tools can handle damage that once required expensive professional services, making preservation accessible for every family.
