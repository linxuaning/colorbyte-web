For adoptees connecting with birth families, old photographs may be the first visual evidence of biological heritage — the face that mirrors their own, the family history they were separated from. Restored photos of birth family members can be among the most profound discoveries of an adoptee's life.

## The Challenge

Adoption reunion photography often involves comparing faces across generations and families — the adoptee looking for resemblance in birth family photos, the birth family seeing features they recognize in an adoptee's photo. This facial comparison requires the highest possible image quality.

## How AI Helps

AI restoration for adoption reunion purposes applies maximum facial detail recovery and optimizes for cross-image facial comparison. The algorithm ensures that distinctive facial features — the elements most relevant to recognizing family resemblance — are as clearly recovered as possible.

## Next Steps

Gather your photographs, scan at the highest resolution available (600 DPI minimum), and visit [PhotoFix](/old-photo-restoration) to start the restoration process. AI tools can handle damage that once required expensive professional services, making preservation accessible for every family.
