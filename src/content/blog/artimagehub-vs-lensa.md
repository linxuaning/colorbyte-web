Lensa AI went viral for its "Magic Avatars" — AI-generated artistic portraits based on selfies. ArtImageHub is a specialized pipeline for old photo restoration. People searching for AI photo tools sometimes encounter both; they solve completely different problems.

---

## What Lensa AI Does

Lensa AI is primarily a **selfie enhancement and AI avatar app:**

**AI Avatars (Magic Avatars):** Upload 10–20 selfies → AI generates artistic portraits of you in various styles. This is generative AI — new images, not edits of existing ones.

**Portrait retouching:** Skin smoothing, face reshaping, eye enhancement, virtual makeup — for modern selfies and portraits.

**Photo editing:** Filters, effects, background blur.

**Platform:** iOS and Android app.

---

## Why Lensa Doesn't Restore Old Photos

**It's designed for modern selfies.** Lensa's tools — smoothing, reshaping, eye enhancement — target contemporary high-resolution smartphone photography. Applied to a degraded 1950s portrait:
- The face detection may fail on heavily degraded faces
- The smoothing tools work on degraded paper texture, not skin
- No scratch, fading, or damage correction

**Magic Avatars generate new images.** Uploading old photos as avatar training input produces AI-generated artistic interpretations — not restorations of the original.

**No historical face reconstruction.** No CodeFormer equivalent. No GFPGAN fading correction.
