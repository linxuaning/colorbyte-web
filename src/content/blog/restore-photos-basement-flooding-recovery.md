# Recovering Family Photos from Basement Flood Damage

Basement flooding is among the most common causes of family photo loss — the box of photos in the corner, the albums on the bottom shelf, the unlabeled collection saved for someday. When flooding hits, quick action can save more photos than most families realize.

## The Challenge

Emergency flood recovery for photos involves immediate drying to prevent mold growth, careful separation of stuck photos before they dry, and documentation of the damage for insurance purposes. Speed is critical — mold begins growing within 24-48 hours.

## How AI Helps

ArtImageHub flood damage recovery can be applied as soon as photos are dried and stable. The algorithm addresses the combination of water stain, silver migration, and drying damage simultaneously, using the most aggressive available recovery protocols for disaster-damaged photos.
