# Restoring Route 66 and Cross-Country Road Trip Photos

Route 66 and cross-country road trip photos document an American mythology — the open road, the motor court motel, the roadside diner, the car packed with children. These images capture both personal family adventure and the landscape of mid-century America.

## The Challenge

Road trip photo collections span multiple rolls of film exposed in varying conditions — desert glare, mountain overcast, urban night. The mixing of radically different exposures across a single trip created collections with wildly varying technical quality.

## How AI Helps

AI batch restoration applies condition-specific processing to each image within a road trip collection, normalizing the technical quality while preserving the different atmospheric qualities of desert, mountain, and urban settings.
