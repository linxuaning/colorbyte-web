Professional genealogists increasingly integrate AI photo restoration into their research methodology — restored photos reveal identifying details that advance research, provide evidence for lineage connections, and create the visual documentation that clients value most.

## The Challenge

Professional genealogy photo restoration follows strict documentation protocols — the original scan, the restoration parameters applied, and the restored result are all documented to maintain research integrity. Restoration is treated as evidence transformation, not just image improvement.

## How AI Helps

AI restoration for professional genealogy use provides detailed documentation of all restoration processes applied to each image. This restoration log satisfies professional genealogical standards for evidence documentation and allows research partners to evaluate the confidence level of recovered details.

## Next Steps

Gather your photographs, scan at the highest resolution available (600 DPI minimum), and visit [PhotoFix](/old-photo-restoration) to start the restoration process. AI tools can handle damage that once required expensive professional services, making preservation accessible for every family.
