# Preserving Portuguese and Azorean American Heritage Photos

Portuguese and Azorean American photos document the maritime heritage of communities centered in New England and California fishing ports. The Festas do Espírito Santo, religious processions, and fishing fleet blessings created rich photographic traditions.

## The Challenge

Maritime community photography shares the salt air damage challenges of all coastal photography, combined with the high-contrast outdoor lighting of outdoor festival and procession photography. Portuguese festas often continued into evening, creating additional low-light challenges.

## How AI Helps

AI restoration trained on coastal religious ceremony photography handles the specific combination of salt damage and outdoor festival lighting. The algorithm addresses both physical deterioration from salt air and the technical challenges of outdoor procession photography.
