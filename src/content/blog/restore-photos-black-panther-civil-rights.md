# Preserving Civil Rights and Social Justice Movement Photos

Civil rights movement photos document one of America's most transformative social upheavals — the marches, the sit-ins, the voter registration drives, and the communities that organized for justice. These images are historical documents as well as family treasures.

## The Challenge

Civil rights movement photos were often taken by community photographers who documented local organizing that never received national media attention. These local movement photos are the most at risk of being lost — there is no institutional archive that holds them.

## How AI Helps

AI restoration treats civil rights movement photos as historically significant documents deserving maximum recovery effort. The algorithm prioritizes recovering legible signs, banners, and faces — the identifying information that makes these images valuable as historical records.
