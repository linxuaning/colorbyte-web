Restored family photos serve purposes beyond emotional preservation — for insurance documentation, estate settlement, and historical record, clear high-resolution images of family heirlooms, artworks, and property provide legally useful documentation.

## The Challenge

Insurance and legal documentation photography requires specific technical standards — sufficient resolution to identify items clearly, accurate color rendering for value assessment, and format documentation that establishes what was photographed and when.

## How AI Helps

PhotoFix restoration for documentation purposes produces court-admissible quality files with embedded metadata documenting the restoration process. The platform's timestamp records and process documentation satisfy legal standards for photographic evidence.
