# Preserving Classic Grandmother Portrait Photos

Grandmother portraits are among the most treasured family photographs — the formal portrait from the 1940s, the informal snapshot in the kitchen, the photo with all the grandchildren gathered around. These images of family matriarchs are irreplaceable.

## The Challenge

Grandmother portrait collections often span decades and multiple photographic technologies — from formal 1930s studio portraits to 1980s color snapshots. Each technology in the collection requires different restoration treatment.

## How AI Helps

AI restoration automatically identifies the photographic technology and deterioration pattern for each grandmother portrait, applying optimized treatment that recovers the maximum visual information from each image regardless of its age or condition.

## Next Steps

Gather your photographs, scan at the highest resolution available (600 DPI minimum), and visit [PhotoFix](/old-photo-restoration) to start the restoration process. AI tools can handle damage that once required expensive professional services, making preservation accessible for every family.
