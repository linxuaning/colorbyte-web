Easter Sunday photos capture the joy of spring renewal — children in new outfits searching for hidden eggs, family dinners after church, and three generations gathered around a holiday table. These annual traditions created reliable photographic records of family growth.

## The Challenge

Spring holiday photos from the mid-20th century often used color film that rendered pastel colors — the traditional palette of Easter clothing — with characteristic shifts. Pinks tended toward magenta, and soft blues became dull over time.

## How AI Helps

AI color restoration algorithms trained on period-accurate color film characteristics effectively recover the soft pastel palette of Easter photos, restoring the delicate colors of spring dresses and decorations to their original freshness.

## Next Steps

Gather your photographs, scan at the highest resolution available (600 DPI minimum), and visit [PhotoFix](/old-photo-restoration) to start the restoration process. AI tools can handle damage that once required expensive professional services, making preservation accessible for every family.
