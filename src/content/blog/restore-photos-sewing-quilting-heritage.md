# Preserving Sewing, Quilting, and Textile Craft Heritage Photos

Quilting and sewing photos document the textile arts that were central to women's community life for generations. The quilting bee, the sewing circle, and the finished quilt presented to a bride or newborn are images of craft, community, and continuity.

## The Challenge

Fabric and textile photos present color restoration challenges — the subtle color variations in quilt patterns and sewn garments require precise color accuracy that deteriorated prints cannot provide. Faded color photos of quilts often look like a single muddy tone.

## How AI Helps

AI textile color restoration trained on historical fabric photography can separate and restore the individual color channels of quilt patterns and sewn garments, recovering the distinct colors of each fabric square or design element that are lost to general fading.

## Next Steps
