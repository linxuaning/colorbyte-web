## Why Wedding Photos Deteriorate

Wedding photographs face unique preservation challenges due to how they were stored and displayed over the decades.

### Common Causes of Wedding Photo Damage

**Display Damage:**
- Prolonged exposure to sunlight in frames
- Heat from display lights
- Humidity in displayed locations
- Dust accumulation on glass

**Storage Issues:**
- Acidic paper in old albums
- Adhesive deterioration
- Basement or attic temperature extremes
- Water damage from leaks
