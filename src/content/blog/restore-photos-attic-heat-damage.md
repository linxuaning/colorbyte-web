# Recovering Photos from Attic Heat and Humidity Damage

Attic storage is among the worst environments for family photos — summer temperatures in unconditioned attics can exceed 130°F, accelerating every chemical deterioration process by orders of magnitude. Yet countless family photo collections were stored in attics for decades.

## The Challenge

Heat damage creates specific deterioration patterns — emulsion becomes brittle and cracks at high temperatures, color dyes fade at accelerated rates, and print paper yellows from heat-induced acid formation. Photos with extensive attic storage show the combined effects of all these processes.

## How AI Helps

AI restoration addresses attic heat damage through multi-layer treatment — emulsion crack repair, accelerated color fading correction, and yellowing reversal applied in the optimal sequence for maximum recovery. Even severely heat-damaged attic photos often yield better results than families expect.
