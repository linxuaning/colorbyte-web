# Preserving Greek Orthodox and Hellenic Heritage Photos

Greek Orthodox community photos document the religious and cultural traditions that defined Greek American life — the elaborate Easter midnight service, the community panegyri festival, the AHEPA organization gatherings. These images preserve Hellenic heritage across generations.

## The Challenge

Greek Orthodox Easter photography is particularly challenging — the midnight service involves candlelight processions and the movement of large crowds in dim church exteriors. These technically demanding conditions produced photos that appear nearly unusable without restoration.

## How AI Helps

AI night photography enhancement trained on candlelit religious ceremonies effectively recovers detail from Orthodox Easter midnight service photos. The algorithm separates candle flame highlights from surrounding darkness, revealing the faces of worshippers in the traditional procession.

## Next Steps

Gather your photographs, scan at the highest resolution available (600 DPI minimum), and visit [PhotoFix](/old-photo-restoration) to start the restoration process. AI tools can handle damage that once required expensive professional services, making preservation accessible for every family.
