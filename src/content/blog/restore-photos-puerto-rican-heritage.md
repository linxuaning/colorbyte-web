# Preserving Puerto Rican Heritage and Community Photos

Puerto Rican community photos document the vibrant cultural life of diaspora communities in New York, Chicago, and beyond — Three Kings Day celebrations, bomba and plena music performances, and the street festivals that brought the island's culture to mainland cities.

## The Challenge

Puerto Rican community celebration photos were typically informal snapshots taken at outdoor street festivals and indoor community events. The mix of bright Puerto Rican flag colors (red, white, blue) and tropical color palette created vivid images that faded particularly noticeably.

## How AI Helps

AI color restoration trained on Caribbean photography accurately recovers the saturated warm tones characteristic of Puerto Rican celebration photography. The vibrant flag colors and tropical palettes respond well to the algorithm's saturated color recovery capabilities.

## Next Steps

Gather your photographs, scan at the highest resolution available (600 DPI minimum), and visit [PhotoFix](/old-photo-restoration) to start the restoration process. AI tools can handle damage that once required expensive professional services, making preservation accessible for every family.
