The complete family history combines images with voice — a restored photograph of a grandparent shown during a recorded oral history interview creates a richer document than either alone. The photo triggers specific memories; the voice gives the photo its meaning.

## The Challenge

Oral history recording with family photos requires preparation — which photos will be shown, what questions will be asked, and how the recordings will be stored and connected to the photos. Planning the oral history session alongside photo restoration maximizes the combined value.

## How AI Helps

AI restoration for oral history use prioritizes photos that will trigger specific memories — the most damaged images of important events often correspond to the most emotionally significant stories. Maximum recovery effort on these key photos enhances the oral history session's depth.

## Next Steps

Gather your photographs, scan at the highest resolution available (600 DPI minimum), and visit [PhotoFix](/old-photo-restoration) to start the restoration process. AI tools can handle damage that once required expensive professional services, making preservation accessible for every family.
