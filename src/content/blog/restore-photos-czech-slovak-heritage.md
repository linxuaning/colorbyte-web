# Preserving Czech and Slovak American Heritage Photos

Czech and Slovak American community photos document the establishment of Bohemian and Slovak cultural institutions in America — the Sokol gymnastic societies, Czech and Slovak Catholic churches, and the community newspapers and mutual aid organizations that supported new immigrants.

## The Challenge

Community organization photos like Sokol gymnastics performances involved large groups in coordinated movement, creating motion blur challenges even for studio photographers. Many Sokol performance photos used the same technical approaches as theater photography.

## How AI Helps

AI motion correction trained on group performance photography effectively recovers sharp images from Sokol and similar organization performance photos. The algorithm handles coordinated group motion differently from random individual motion, applying appropriate correction.
