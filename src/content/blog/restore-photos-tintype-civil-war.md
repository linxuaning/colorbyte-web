# Preserving Civil War Era Tintype Photos

Civil War tintype photos are among the most historically significant family photographs in American collections — soldiers photographed before battle, family portraits sent to the front, and the documentary record of the nation's most devastating conflict. Many soldiers carried tintypes to their deaths.

## The Challenge

Tintypes are metal-based photographs that resist some damage but are vulnerable to rust, scratching, and the specific deterioration patterns of collodion emulsion. Many Civil War tintypes show significant rust damage that covers portions of the image.

## How AI Helps

AI restoration handles rust damage on tintype photographs using inpainting algorithms trained on metallic substrate imagery. The algorithm reconstructs image content beneath rust corrosion, recovering faces from tintypes that appeared completely obscured.
