# Preserving Class Reunion Photos Across the Decades

Class reunion photos document the reunion of people whose youthful faces may only survive in preserved yearbooks and reunion albums — the 10-year reunion where everyone still looks young, the 25-year reunion where middle age has arrived, the 50-year reunion where survival itself is celebrated.

## The Challenge

Reunion photos capture the full range of adult life changes within a single group photograph — different aging, different fashion choices, different life circumstances. These candid social photos require sensitive enhancement that respects natural aging.

## How AI Helps

AI portrait enhancement for reunion photos respects the authentic aging of subjects while recovering detail from technically imperfect social photography. The enhancement improves photographic quality without altering the genuine appearance of the people photographed.
