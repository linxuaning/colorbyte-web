Wedding and anniversary albums are the most carefully preserved family photos — kept in embossed leather covers, stored in climate-controlled closets, handled with white gloves. Yet even the most carefully kept albums accumulate damage over decades.

## The Challenge

Professional wedding photography used the best available consumer photography technology for each era, but even professional-grade printing from the 1960s-1980s deteriorates significantly. Wedding albums from these decades show characteristic color shift and fading.

## How AI Helps

AI restoration applies wedding photography-specific processing that accounts for the typical photographic conditions of wedding venues — church sanctuary lighting, outdoor reception settings, and the mixed lighting of evening celebrations — recovering accurate colors from each setting.

## Next Steps

Gather your photographs, scan at the highest resolution available (600 DPI minimum), and visit [PhotoFix](/old-photo-restoration) to start the restoration process. AI tools can handle damage that once required expensive professional services, making preservation accessible for every family.
