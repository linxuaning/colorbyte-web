# Preserving Family Bible Records and Heritage Photos

Family Bibles served as the original family photo albums — small prints, portraits, and document photos preserved between the pages of the family's most sacred book. These photos carry both genealogical and spiritual significance, often representing the oldest surviving family images.

## The Challenge

Photos stored between Bible pages developed distinctive damage patterns from the acidic paper of older Bibles and from being pressed flat for decades. The characteristic Bible crease pattern — multiple parallel horizontal folds — identifies photos that spent years stored in this way.

## How AI Helps

AI restoration handles Bible storage damage through specialized fold crease repair algorithms that reconstruct image content across the distinctive parallel fold pattern. Combined with acid yellowing correction, these algorithms recover photos from one of the most common family heirloom storage conditions.
