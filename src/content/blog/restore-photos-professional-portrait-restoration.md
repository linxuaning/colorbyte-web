Professional portrait studio photos from earlier eras were taken with the best available photographic technology, but even professional-grade photography from the mid-20th century deteriorates significantly over decades. The investment families made in professional photography deserves to be recovered.

## The Challenge

Professional studio photography used specific technical approaches — large format cameras, controlled lighting, and professional printing that produce distinctive image characteristics. Understanding these characteristics allows restoration to be tailored to the specific technical profile of professional portrait work.

## How AI Helps

AI restoration trained on professional portrait photography from different eras applies studio-appropriate treatment that respects the technical qualities of professional work. The restoration recovers the sharpness, tonal range, and color accuracy that professional photographers originally achieved.

## Next Steps

Gather your photographs, scan at the highest resolution available (600 DPI minimum), and visit [PhotoFix](/old-photo-restoration) to start the restoration process. AI tools can handle damage that once required expensive professional services, making preservation accessible for every family.
