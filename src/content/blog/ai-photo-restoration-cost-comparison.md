## What Are the Main Pricing Models in AI Photo Restoration?

The market has settled into four basic structures:

**Subscription (monthly or annual)** — Tools like Topaz Photo AI ($199/year), Luminar Neo ($119/year), and Adobe Photoshop ($263/year) charge ongoing fees. They make sense for professional photographers using these tools daily but are expensive for occasional family use.

**Credit packs** — You buy a bundle of credits that expire after 30 to 90 days. Each photo costs one or more credits. This model punishes infrequent users who let credits expire before using them.

**Per-photo one-time** — You pay once for each restoration, with no recurring charge. Quality varies by provider.
