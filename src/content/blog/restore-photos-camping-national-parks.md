# Preserving National Park and Camping Family Photos

National park photos document America's grandest landscapes alongside family memories — children at the Grand Canyon rim, families at Yellowstone geysers, tents in Yosemite Valley. These images combine nature photography with personal family history.

## The Challenge

Wilderness and camping photos faced exposure challenges from dramatic lighting conditions — deep forest shade, bright mountain snow, backlit canyon scenes. Consumer cameras of earlier eras could only properly expose for one of these conditions at a time.

## How AI Helps

AI tone mapping trained on landscape photography handles the extreme dynamic range of wilderness scenes. The algorithm recovers shadow detail in forest shots and sky detail in mountain panoramas, delivering the full visual experience that family cameras could not originally capture.

## Next Steps

Gather your photographs, scan at the highest resolution available (600 DPI minimum), and visit [PhotoFix](/old-photo-restoration) to start the restoration process. AI tools can handle damage that once required expensive professional services, making preservation accessible for every family.
