# Restoring Photos Discovered During Home Renovation

Home renovation projects regularly uncover forgotten family photos — tucked into wall cavities during construction, hidden under flooring, or sealed in attic spaces. These discovered photos may belong to the current owner's ancestors or to completely unrelated previous residents.

## The Challenge

Photos discovered during renovation are typically in conditions reflecting years of concealment — compressed, folded, exposed to construction materials, and often showing the physical damage of being enclosed in building materials for decades.

## How AI Helps

AI restoration for renovation-discovered photos applies maximum recovery algorithms, treating these images as potentially irreplaceable historical documents regardless of whether their subjects are identifiable. Many renovation-discovered photos have been connected to family histories years after their discovery.

## Next Steps

Gather your photographs, scan at the highest resolution available (600 DPI minimum), and visit [old photo restoration tool](/old-photo-restoration) to start the restoration process. AI tools can handle damage that once required expensive professional services, making preservation accessible for every family.
