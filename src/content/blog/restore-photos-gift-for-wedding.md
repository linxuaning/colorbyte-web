A restored family photo makes among the most meaningful wedding gifts — the great-grandparents' wedding portrait brought back to vibrant life, the marriage photo that started the family line the newlyweds are continuing. These gifts connect weddings to the chain of family that made them possible.

## The Challenge

Wedding gift restoration works best when the giver knows which family photos are most meaningful to the recipient. The most powerful gifts are often the photos that have always been treasured but were too damaged to display properly.

## How AI Helps

PhotoFix gift restoration service includes premium presentation options — archival printing, framing coordination, and gift packaging that presents the restored photo as the precious gift it represents. The combination of emotional significance and technical excellence creates a gift that will be treasured for generations.
