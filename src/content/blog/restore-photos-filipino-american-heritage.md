# Preserving Filipino American Heritage and Community Photos

Filipino American community photos document one of the largest Asian American communities — the pensionados who came as students, the farmworkers of the Delano grape strike, the nurses who transformed American healthcare, and the vibrant bayanihan community spirit.

## The Challenge

Filipino American photos span a wide range of tropical and American storage conditions. Photos brought from the Philippines show the humidity damage of tropical storage, while American-stored photos show typical consumer photography deterioration.

## How AI Helps

AI restoration handles the mixed tropical and temperate damage patterns of Filipino American collections through climate-aware processing. The algorithm applies tropical humidity protocols to Philippines-origin photos while using standard American photo restoration for American-stored images.

## Next Steps

Gather your photographs, scan at the highest resolution available (600 DPI minimum), and visit [restore old photos online](/old-photo-restoration) to start the restoration process. AI tools can handle damage that once required expensive professional services, making preservation accessible for every family.
