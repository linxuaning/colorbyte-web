# Preserving Honeymoon Travel Photos from the 1950s, 1960s, and 1970s

Postwar honeymoon travel — Niagara Falls, Miami Beach, Hawaii, and eventually Europe — documented the aspirations of middle-class Americans discovering tourism. These travel photos combine personal milestone with historical record.

## The Challenge

Travel photos from this era were captured on a wide variety of film types as couples experimented with new photographic technologies. Kodachrome slides, color negative film, and even remaining black-and-white prints coexist in typical honeymoon photo collections.

## How AI Helps

AI restoration processes all photographic formats within a unified workflow, applying format-appropriate treatment while ensuring consistent tonal quality across the final collection. Mixed-format honeymoon albums become coherent visual narratives.
