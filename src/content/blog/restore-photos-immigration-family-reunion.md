# Restoring Photos for Immigration Family Reunions

Immigration family separations create photographic disconnections — family members living across borders may not have seen each other in decades, with old photographs as the only visual connection. Restored photos bridge this physical separation with vivid visual memories.

## The Challenge

Cross-border family reunification photography often involves photos from very different photographic traditions — images taken in Mexico, El Salvador, China, or India have different technical characteristics and deterioration patterns than American-stored family photos.

## How AI Helps

AI restoration handles diverse photographic traditions within a single workflow, applying culturally appropriate treatment to photos from different national photography traditions. The restored images normalize across traditions while respecting the distinctive qualities of each photographic culture.

## Next Steps

Gather your photographs, scan at the highest resolution available (600 DPI minimum), and visit [PhotoFix](/old-photo-restoration) to start the restoration process. AI tools can handle damage that once required expensive professional services, making preservation accessible for every family.
