Wedding reception photos capture unguarded joy — the first dance, the father-daughter dance, the toast from the best friend, the grandmother dancing with the groom. These candid moments often tell more about a family than the formal ceremony photos.

## The Challenge

Reception dancing photos are technically challenging: motion blur from dancing subjects, mixed candlelight and flash lighting, and the social chaos of celebration all conspired against consumer cameras of earlier eras.

## How AI Helps

AI motion deblur trained on dance photography effectively recovers subject sharpness from candid reception photos. The algorithm distinguishes intentional background blur (bokeh) from unintentional camera shake, preserving depth of field while recovering subject sharpness.

## Next Steps

Gather your photographs, scan at the highest resolution available (600 DPI minimum), and visit [PhotoFix](/old-photo-restoration) to start the restoration process. AI tools can handle damage that once required expensive professional services, making preservation accessible for every family.
