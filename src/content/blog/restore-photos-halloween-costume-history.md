# Restoring Vintage Halloween Costume and Celebration Photos

Vintage Halloween photos capture a simpler era of handmade costumes and neighborhood trick-or-treating. Children in ghost sheets, homemade witch hats, and cardboard robot costumes document childhood creativity and community traditions that shaped generations.

## The Challenge

Halloween photos were often informal snapshots taken in low evening light with consumer cameras. Porch lighting created orange color casts. Early color film rendered the natural autumn palette with poor accuracy, often making warm fall colors appear muddy.

## How AI Helps

AI white balance correction specifically addresses the orange color cast from incandescent porch lighting, recovering accurate skin tones and costume colors. Autumn scene training data helps the algorithm properly render fall foliage backgrounds.
