A restored family photo exists in a hierarchy of preservation — the original print, the high-resolution digital scan, the restored digital file, and the backup copies in multiple locations. Cloud storage provides the geographic redundancy that ensures photos survive any single disaster.

## The Challenge

Cloud storage for family photos involves format choices that affect long-term accessibility — proprietary formats may become unreadable as software changes, while open standards like TIFF and JPEG remain readable indefinitely. Storage strategy should prioritize format longevity.

## How AI Helps

AI restoration outputs in multiple archival formats simultaneously — the restored image is delivered as a full-resolution TIFF for archival storage, a high-quality JPEG for sharing and printing, and metadata-embedded files that document the restoration process and original condition.
