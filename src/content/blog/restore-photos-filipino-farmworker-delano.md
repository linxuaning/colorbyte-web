Filipino farmworker photos document the labor organizing that transformed agricultural labor relations in California — the Delano Grape Strike that Filipino workers initiated in 1965, and the United Farm Workers movement that Larry Itliong and Cesar Chavez led together.

## The Challenge

Labor movement photos were often taken by movement photographers in difficult field conditions — strong sun, dusty environments, and the logistical challenges of documenting strikes and organizing drives. These documentary photos show the challenges of fieldwork photography.

## How AI Helps

AI restoration trained on documentary photography handles the harsh outdoor conditions of farmworker photographs. Detail enhancement recovers faces and signs from protest photos, preserving the record of a labor movement that changed American agricultural practices.
