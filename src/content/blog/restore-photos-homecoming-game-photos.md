# Preserving Homecoming Game and Court Photos

Homecoming photos document an American high school institution — the homecoming court in formal dress on the football field, the parade floats, the game, and the dance. These community celebration photos capture high school life at its most ceremonial.

## The Challenge

Homecoming outdoor event photography faced the same challenges as other outdoor event photography — stadium lighting at night, variable weather conditions, and the motion of parade floats and halftime performances creating technical challenges.

## How AI Helps

AI restoration applies sports venue and outdoor event photography algorithms to homecoming photos, handling both daytime parade photography and night football game photography with appropriate technical correction for each lighting environment.
