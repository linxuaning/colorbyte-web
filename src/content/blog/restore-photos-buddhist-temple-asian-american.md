# Preserving Buddhist Temple and Asian American Heritage Photos

Buddhist temple and Asian American community photos document the cultural institutions that helped immigrant families maintain heritage while building new lives in America. Temple celebrations, community festivals, and cultural education programs created rich photographic records.

## The Challenge

Temple interior photography faced the same low-light challenges as other religious photography, with the additional complexity of incense smoke that settled on print surfaces over decades of temple storage. Many temple archive photos show a haze characteristic of incense contamination.

## How AI Helps

AI restoration algorithms trained on materials science effectively separate incense smoke contamination from photographic image content. The haze removal approach is similar to that used for fog and smoke in landscape photography, recovering the clarity of temple interior photos.
