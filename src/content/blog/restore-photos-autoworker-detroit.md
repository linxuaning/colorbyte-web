Auto workers built the middle class. Photos from Ford, GM, and Chrysler plant families document the union halls, company picnics, and assembly line pride that defined Midwest working-class life for generations.

## The Challenge

Photos stored in Michigan basements and garages face humidity challenges from the Great Lakes climate. Many show water ring damage from flooding or pipe leaks. Factory-adjacent storage exposed prints to oil mist and metallic particulates.

## How AI Helps

AI tools address the water tide mark damage common in basement-stored photos by modeling image content beneath the ring stain. The restoration distinguishes stain patterns from photographic content, recovering details that once appeared permanently obscured.

## Next Steps

Gather your photographs, scan at the highest resolution available (600 DPI minimum), and visit [PhotoFix](/old-photo-restoration) to start the restoration process. AI tools can handle damage that once required expensive professional services, making preservation accessible for every family.
