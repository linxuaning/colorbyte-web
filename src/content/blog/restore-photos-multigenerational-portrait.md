Multigenerational family portraits — the four-generation photo with great-grandmother, grandmother, mother, and newborn — are among the most treasured family images. These rare compositions that span decades of family time deserve the highest priority preservation effort.

## The Challenge

Four-generation photos are among the most carefully composed and taken family images, but the subjects' varied ages create exposure challenges. The pale skin of elderly subjects and the delicate features of infants require different exposure settings that consumer cameras could not simultaneously optimize.

## How AI Helps

AI restoration applies differential subject enhancement to multigenerational portraits, optimizing face detail independently for each age group represented. The result recovers the complete portrait with each generation's features clearly visible and naturally rendered.

## Next Steps

Gather your photographs, scan at the highest resolution available (600 DPI minimum), and visit [PhotoFix](/old-photo-restoration) to start the restoration process. AI tools can handle damage that once required expensive professional services, making preservation accessible for every family.
