Mexican American community photos document the rich cultural heritage of families with roots stretching back before the U.S.-Mexico border was drawn. Día de los Muertos altars, quinceañera celebrations, and barrio community life are captured in images that span generations.

## The Challenge

Mexican American photo collections often span from pre-photographic-era heirlooms through contemporary digital images. Pre-photographic-era items like hand-painted portraits and tin-type images coexist with modern photos in family collections.

## How AI Helps

AI restoration platform handles the complete range of photographic technologies while also offering enhancement for pre-photographic portrait formats. Hand-painted portraits and early tintype images receive specialized treatment appropriate to their unique preservation needs.

## Next Steps

Gather your photographs, scan at the highest resolution available (600 DPI minimum), and visit [PhotoFix](/old-photo-restoration) to start the restoration process. AI tools can handle damage that once required expensive professional services, making preservation accessible for every family.
