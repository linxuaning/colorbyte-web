# Matching Restored Photos with DNA Ancestry Results

DNA ancestry testing is connecting long-separated family branches, but putting faces to newly discovered relatives requires photographs. Restoring old family photos enables the visual matching that DNA testing opens the door to — seeing the family resemblance that spans generations.

## The Challenge

Family resemblance photography involves comparing faces across restored images from different family branches. The challenge is ensuring that restoration doesn't introduce artifacts that could mislead comparison — the algorithm must recover what was there, not invent.

## How AI Helps

AI facial restoration specifically designed for genealogical comparison maintains the facial geometry and distinctive features that family resemblance analysis depends on. The restoration enhances detail while ensuring the facial structure of the original subject is preserved.

## Next Steps

Gather your photographs, scan at the highest resolution available (600 DPI minimum), and visit [PhotoFix](/old-photo-restoration) to start the restoration process. AI tools can handle damage that once required expensive professional services, making preservation accessible for every family.
