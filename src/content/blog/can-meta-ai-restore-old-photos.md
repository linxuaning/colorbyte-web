Meta AI is Meta's conversational AI assistant — available across Facebook, Instagram, WhatsApp, and as a standalone product. Built on Llama models. If you're wondering whether it can restore old family photographs:

**Meta AI cannot restore old photographs.** It's a conversational assistant that can discuss images, answer questions, and generate new images — but it cannot edit, restore, or modify existing photographs.

---

## What Meta AI Can Do

**Conversation about photos:** Meta AI can analyze photos you share and describe what it sees.

**Image generation (Imagine):** Meta AI includes image generation capability — creating new images from text descriptions. This is generation, not restoration.

**Advice:** Meta AI can recommend photo restoration tools and explain approaches.

**What it cannot do:** Apply restoration models to your photos. Meta AI doesn't run CodeFormer, GFPGAN, or any image-to-image restoration pipeline. It cannot remove scratches, fix fading, or reconstruct face detail in your uploaded photos.

---

## The Irony: Meta Published Key Restoration Research

Meta's AI research division (FAIR) has actually published significant work on image restoration and enhancement. However, these research models are not available through Meta AI's consumer products (Facebook, Instagram, WhatsApp).

The consumer Meta AI products are conversational assistants and image generators — not image restoration tools.
