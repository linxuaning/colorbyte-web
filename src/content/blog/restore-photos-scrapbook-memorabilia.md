# Preserving Family Scrapbooks and Photo Memorabilia

Family scrapbooks are the most intimate archives — photos mixed with ticket stubs, pressed flowers, letters, and clippings that together tell the story of a life. Restoring scrapbook photos means preserving both the images and their emotional context.

## The Challenge

Scrapbook photos were often adhered with early adhesives that migrated into the photographic emulsion, causing staining that appears as yellow or brown spots within the image area. The adhesive damage is distinct from ordinary fading.

## How AI Helps

AI restoration identifies adhesive migration staining as a distinct damage type from ordinary deterioration, applying targeted inpainting that removes stain patterns while preserving photographic content. The restored photos can be safely digitized alongside the scrapbook's other ephemera.

## Next Steps

Gather your photographs, scan at the highest resolution available (600 DPI minimum), and visit [PhotoFix](/old-photo-restoration) to start the restoration process. AI tools can handle damage that once required expensive professional services, making preservation accessible for every family.
