# Preserving Iranian and Persian American Heritage Photos

Iranian American photos document the before-and-after of revolutionary displacement — the formal studio portraits of Tehran's middle class before 1979, the documentation of exile, and the new family moments built in Los Angeles and beyond. The contrast between then and now gives these images profound meaning.

## The Challenge

Pre-revolution Iranian formal photography used studio techniques influenced by both European and traditional Persian aesthetics. These formal portraits deteriorate differently from American studio photography, requiring culturally-informed restoration approaches.

## How AI Helps

AI restoration trained on Middle Eastern studio photography accurately addresses the specific deterioration patterns of Iranian formal portraits. The restoration respects the formal compositional elements that characterize Persian portrait photography traditions.
