Korean American community photos document the remarkable story of Korean immigration — the families who came after the Korean War, the vibrant Korean church communities, and the educational and economic achievement that characterized the Korean American experience.

## The Challenge

Korean American photo collections typically span from formal studio portraits brought from Korea to American-born children's snapshots. Korean studio photography of the 1950s-1970s used printing technologies with specific fading characteristics that require specialized restoration.

## How AI Helps

AI restoration database includes color profiles for Korean studio photography from major eras, allowing accurate color restoration for formal family portraits brought from Korea. The systematic color shift of Korean printing technologies from specific decades is addressed through targeted correction.

## Next Steps

Gather your photographs, scan at the highest resolution available (600 DPI minimum), and visit [PhotoFix](/old-photo-restoration) to start the restoration process. AI tools can handle damage that once required expensive professional services, making preservation accessible for every family.
