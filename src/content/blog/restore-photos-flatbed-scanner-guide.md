# The Complete Guide to Flatbed Scanning for Photo Restoration

A quality flatbed scanner is the essential tool for serious family photo preservation — producing archival-quality digital files that capture every detail in original prints. Understanding scanner settings ensures that restoration services receive files with enough information to work from.

## The Challenge

Optimal flatbed scanning for restoration requires specific settings — 600 DPI minimum for standard prints, 1200 DPI for small prints and documents, 2400+ DPI for medium format film. Color profile settings, bit depth, and file format choices all affect the quality of the base file.

## How AI Helps
