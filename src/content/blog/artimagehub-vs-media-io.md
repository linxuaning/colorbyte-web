## Where Media.io Falls Short for Old Photo Restoration

**Jack of all trades.** Media.io's photo restorer is one feature among dozens. The AI models are general-purpose — they handle light damage and basic enhancement, but struggle with heavy degradation, deep scratches, or severely faded prints.

**Face reconstruction is shallow.** Media.io's face enhancement works on mildly blurred faces. For heavily degraded 1940s–1960s faces where features are barely visible, it doesn't have CodeFormer-level reconstruction depth.

**Subscription model adds up.** The free tier adds watermarks and limits resolution. To get clean outputs, you need a monthly subscription — $3.99/month means you're paying more than ArtImageHub's one-time fee within two months.

**Inconsistent quality.** As a general toolkit, results vary significantly. Simple enhancement (brightness, sharpness) works well. Deep restoration (reconstructing missing facial features, repairing torn sections) often produces artifacts.
