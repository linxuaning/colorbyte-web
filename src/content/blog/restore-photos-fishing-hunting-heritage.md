# Preserving Fishing and Hunting Heritage Photos

Fishing and hunting photos document a specific male tradition of mentorship and outdoor heritage — the first deer, the big catch, the father teaching his son to cast. These images carry the weight of passed-down skills and knowledge about the natural world.

## The Challenge

Outdoor hunting and fishing photos were typically taken in challenging conditions — dawn light at a hunting blind, the shade of a riverbank, the glare of open water. Consumer cameras of earlier eras handled few of these conditions well.

## How AI Helps

AI outdoor lighting correction uses scene recognition to identify outdoor photography context and apply environment-appropriate corrections. Dawn light, open water glare, and forest shade each receive specialized treatment that preserves the authentic outdoor atmosphere.
