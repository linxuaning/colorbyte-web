Science fair and academic achievement photos capture intellectual pride — the child standing next to a project that took weeks to build, the award ceremony, the teacher's recognition. These images document the development of curiosity and achievement.

## The Challenge

Academic event photography was typically informal snapshot photography taken in school gymnasium or cafeteria lighting. Fluorescent lighting created the characteristic green color cast that consumer film rendered poorly, affecting the tonal accuracy of achievement photos.

## How AI Helps

AI white balance correction trained on fluorescent lighting environments effectively removes the green color cast characteristic of school event photography. This single correction often transforms flat, sickly-looking achievement photos into vivid records of childhood accomplishment.

## Next Steps

Gather your photographs, scan at the highest resolution available (600 DPI minimum), and visit [PhotoFix](/old-photo-restoration) to start the restoration process. AI tools can handle damage that once required expensive professional services, making preservation accessible for every family.
