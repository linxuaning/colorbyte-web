# Preserving Thanksgiving Family Reunion Photos

Thanksgiving photos capture the essence of American family life — the laden table, the multigenerational gathering, the football game in the backyard. These annual traditions produced a consistent photographic record of family change across decades.

## The Challenge

Indoor holiday photography from the 1950s-1980s was heavily influenced by the warm color cast of incandescent lighting. Film of that era also rendered warm colors (browns, oranges) with limited accuracy, creating an overall amber cast in holiday dining photos.

## How AI Helps

AI white balance and color correction algorithms trained on indoor holiday photography effectively neutralize incandescent color casts while preserving the genuine warmth of holiday gatherings. The results recover skin tones and food colors that tell the story of the celebration.

## Next Steps

Gather your photographs, scan at the highest resolution available (600 DPI minimum), and visit [restore old photos online](/old-photo-restoration) to start the restoration process. AI tools can handle damage that once required expensive professional services, making preservation accessible for every family.
