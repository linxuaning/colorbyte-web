# Restoring School Yearbook and Class Photo Heritage

School class photos created annual records of childhood — the kindergarten lineup in September, the class photo with the teacher, the yearbook portrait. These images allow families to trace a child's growth through their entire school career.

## The Challenge

School photo packages were produced using industrial volume printing processes that prioritized cost over longevity. The papers and inks used in school photo processing deteriorate faster than professional portrait photography, creating collections that fade within decades.

## How AI Helps

AI restoration accounts for the specific deterioration patterns of school photo printing technologies. The restoration algorithm applies more aggressive recovery to high-volume school prints while maintaining the personal character of individual student portraits.
