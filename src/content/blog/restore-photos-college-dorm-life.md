College photos capture a transformative period — dorm rooms decorated with posters, late-night study sessions, campus protests, and the friendships that define adult life. These images document the intellectual and social awakening of young adulthood.

## The Challenge

College photo collections are notoriously haphazardly stored — shoved in boxes after graduation, moved through multiple apartments, and finally forgotten in attic storage. Multiple moves and storage in non-climate-controlled spaces creates diverse deterioration patterns.

## How AI Helps

AI restoration algorithms handle diverse deterioration patterns in a single workflow by analyzing each image independently before applying targeted corrections. This allows a single scan session to yield dramatically different restoration treatments for photos from the same collection.

## Next Steps

Gather your photographs, scan at the highest resolution available (600 DPI minimum), and visit [PhotoFix](/old-photo-restoration) to start the restoration process. AI tools can handle damage that once required expensive professional services, making preservation accessible for every family.
