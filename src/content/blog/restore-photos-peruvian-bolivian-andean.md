# Preserving Peruvian, Bolivian, and Andean Heritage Photos

Andean American photos document the heritage of families from Peru, Bolivia, and the Andes mountains — the Inti Raymi solar festival, traditional textile arts, and the indigenous cultural traditions that survived colonization and continue in American diaspora communities.

## The Challenge

High-altitude Andean photography presents specific preservation challenges — the intense UV radiation at altitude accelerated silver oxidation in photos stored in highland communities. Photos brought from altitude to sea-level American storage show the effects of this pre-immigration damage.

## How AI Helps

AI restoration trained on altitude-UV damage patterns addresses the specific silver oxidation characteristics of high-altitude storage damage, recovering image content that sea-level restoration approaches might miss.
