Military heritage preservation combines photographs with service records to create complete documentation of a veteran's service — the enlistment photo, the unit portraits, the discharge papers, the medals, and the post-service life. This comprehensive archive honors service completely.

## The Challenge

Military service record photographs exist in both personal family collections and government archives. Cross-referencing family photos with official records often reveals identifying details — unit numbers, dates, and locations — that enhance both personal and official documentation.

## How AI Helps

AI restoration for military heritage archives includes metadata extraction that identifies service-specific details visible in photos — uniform insignia, unit markings, and period details that connect personal photos with official military records.

## Next Steps

Gather your photographs, scan at the highest resolution available (600 DPI minimum), and visit [PhotoFix](/old-photo-restoration) to start the restoration process. AI tools can handle damage that once required expensive professional services, making preservation accessible for every family.
