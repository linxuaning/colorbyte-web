# Building a Visual Family Tree with Restored Photos

A visual family tree combining restored photos with genealogical data creates the most engaging family history format — each ancestor's name connected to their face, their vital dates documented alongside their portrait. This format makes family history accessible to children and adults alike.

## The Challenge

Visual family tree photography requires consistent portrait orientation and scale across generations of photos — the 19th-century cabinet card portrait must be formatted consistently with the 1960s snapshot. Normalization across photographic eras is the primary technical challenge.

## How AI Helps

AI restoration normalizes portraits across photographic eras for visual family tree use — converting diverse formats, orientations, and scales to consistent portrait specifications while maintaining each image's distinctive historical character. The result is a visually coherent family tree that spans generations.

## Next Steps

Gather your photographs, scan at the highest resolution available (600 DPI minimum), and visit [PhotoFix](/old-photo-restoration) to start the restoration process. AI tools can handle damage that once required expensive professional services, making preservation accessible for every family.
