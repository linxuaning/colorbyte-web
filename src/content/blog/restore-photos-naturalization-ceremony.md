# Preserving Naturalization and Citizenship Ceremony Photos

Naturalization ceremony photos document the profound moment when an immigrant became American — the oath, the judge, the flag, the new citizen's expression of arrival after a long journey. These images capture the promise of America at its most personal.

## The Challenge

Naturalization ceremony photos were often taken in courthouse environments with challenging fluorescent or mixed lighting. The formal occasion meant family members brought their best cameras, but courthouse lighting defeated consumer equipment.

## How AI Helps

AI restoration handles courthouse fluorescent lighting correction while preserving the formality and significance of naturalization ceremony photos. The enhanced images restore the dignity of one of American life's most meaningful milestones.

## Next Steps

Gather your photographs, scan at the highest resolution available (600 DPI minimum), and visit [restore old photos online](/old-photo-restoration) to start the restoration process. AI tools can handle damage that once required expensive professional services, making preservation accessible for every family.
