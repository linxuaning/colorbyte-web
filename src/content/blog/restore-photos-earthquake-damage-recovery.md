# Recovering Family Photos from Earthquake Damage

Earthquake damage to family photo collections typically involves physical impact damage — photos crushed by fallen shelves, punctured by debris, torn by structural collapse. The physical character of earthquake damage creates different restoration challenges than chemical deterioration.

## The Challenge

Physical impact damage from earthquakes affects photos with punctures, tears, crushing, and contamination from structural materials. These damage types require the physical damage repair protocols — inpainting for tears and punctures, surface cleaning for contamination.
