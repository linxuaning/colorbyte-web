# Preserving Vietnamese American Heritage and Refugee Photos

Vietnamese American photos carry the weight of profound historical experience — the elegant formal portraits from pre-war Saigon, the refugee camp documentation, and the new family moments built in California, Texas, and Virginia. Each collection tells a story of loss and rebuilding.

## The Challenge

Pre-war Vietnamese formal photography used French-influenced studio techniques that produced images with specific preservation characteristics. These elegant portraits face different deterioration patterns than the informal snapshots taken in refugee camps and early American life.

## How AI Helps

AI restoration applies era and technology-appropriate processing to each image in a Vietnamese American collection, recognizing the differences between pre-war studio photography, refugee documentation photos, and American snapshot photography within the same family archive.
