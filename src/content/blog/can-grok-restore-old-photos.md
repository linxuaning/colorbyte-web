# Can Grok (xAI) Restore Old Photos?

Grok is xAI's conversational AI assistant, available through X (formerly Twitter) and as a standalone product. If you're wondering whether Grok can restore old, faded, or damaged family photographs:

**Grok cannot restore old photographs.** Grok is a conversational AI that can analyze and discuss images but cannot edit, restore, or output modified image files.

---

## What Grok Can Do with Photos

**Image understanding:** Grok can look at an uploaded photo and describe what it sees, analyze damage, and explain what type of restoration might help.

**Advice:** Grok can recommend tools and approaches for photo restoration.

**Image generation (Aurora):** Grok includes an image generation model (Aurora) for creating new images from text descriptions — but this is generation, not restoration of existing photographs.

**What Grok cannot do:** Edit an existing image, apply restoration models, remove scratches, correct fading, or reconstruct face detail. Grok's output is text and generated images — not edited versions of your uploaded photos.
