# AI Photo Restoration vs Manual Professional Retouching

The choice between AI restoration and manual professional retouching depends on the photo's importance, the nature and severity of the damage, and the budget available. Understanding where each approach excels helps families make optimal choices for their specific situations.

## The Challenge

AI restoration excels at consistent, high-quality treatment of large collections, predictable damage types like fading and color shift, and photos where good quality is needed but perfection is not required. Manual retouching excels at complex reconstruction work, photos with significant missing areas, and the most important single images.

## How AI Helps

PhotoFix combines AI efficiency with human review options — the AI handles the volume work, while expert human reviewers can provide additional enhancement for the most important photos in a collection. This hybrid approach delivers the best practical outcome for most family photo projects.
