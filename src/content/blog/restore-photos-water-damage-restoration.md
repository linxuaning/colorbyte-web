# Recovering Water-Damaged Family Photos with AI

Water damage is among the most common disaster damage affecting family photo collections — floods, pipe leaks, and hurricane storm surge have destroyed millions of family photos. But water-damaged photos are often more recoverable than they appear.

## The Challenge

Water damage presents different challenges depending on severity — tide marks from partial submersion respond to targeted stain removal, while photos that were fully submerged and dried may show silver migration and emulsion lifting that requires more extensive reconstruction.

## How AI Helps

ArtImageHub water damage recovery protocols address both stain removal and emulsion reconstruction. The algorithm models the original image beneath water tide marks and reconstructs lifting emulsion sections, recovering images that appear completely destroyed by water damage.
