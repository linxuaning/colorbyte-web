## Grading Criteria

- **Title:** Keyword in title, under 60 chars, unique, includes brand
- **Meta description:** Action-oriented, 140–160 chars, keyword present, CTR hook
- **H1:** Matches primary keyword, one H1 per page
- **Canonical:** Set and matches page URL
- **Target keyword:** Appears in title + description + H1

- **Risk:** Three pages all targeting the "free photo restoration online" keyword cluster with potentially overlapping title/description
- **Specific overlap:**
  - `/free-photo-restoration`: "Free Photo Restoration | Restore Old Photos Online Free - No Signup"
  - `/restore-old-photos-free`: "Restore Old Photos Free | AI Photo Restoration - No Signup"
  - `/restore-photos-online`: "Restore Photos Online Free | AI Photo Restoration in 30 Seconds"
- **Problem:** Titles are near-identical. Google will de-rank all three in favor of the one with most backlinks; the others get no organic traffic.
- **Recommendation:** Pick ONE primary page for "free photo restoration" keyword → set canonical pointing to it from the others, OR differentiate them clearly (e.g., one is mobile-focused, one is desktop, one is the process guide)
- **Grade:** ❌ CRITICAL — deduplicate these pages
